  Formal methods and theory  
  The research of the Formal Methods and Theory Group includes functional programming, formal analysis of programming languages, formal methods in software engineering and Fork algebras. The group investigates how formal specification can supplement software development and how it may help to clarify the semantics of notations (i.e. analysis and design languages, programming languages) and of concepts too.

  Research results transfer  
The research results of this group are currently being applied in the construction of support tools for the process of software development based on models. These tools (e.g. ePlatero) adhere to the MDD (Model Driven Development) philosophy and allow the integration of different specification languages (UML, OCL, Z).
