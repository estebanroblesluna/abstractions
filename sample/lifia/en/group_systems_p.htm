Groupware Systems

Groupware applications allow a group of user to communicate with each other and work cooperatively. By this means, the group can now coordinate their activities, solve problems, edit documents and diagrams, and so on. These capabilities are usually based on electronic services such as emails, video-conference, chat.
As the popularity of the Internet continues to grow, millions of user are connected to work, learn, or just communicate. Even more, several organizations are now integrating groupware technologies into their working environments. This situation requires new tools, environments and metholodogies to improve the way users collaborate to perform their tasks.
Collaborative environment offer different ways of collaboration, such as synchronic or asynchronic communication, face to face or remote work,etc.
