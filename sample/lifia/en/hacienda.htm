NATIONAL TREASURY DEPARTMENT
The National University of La Plata and the National Government have signed an agreement to provide technical and methodological support to the National Treasury Department. LIFIA is providing assistance to develop a system for drawing up and administrating the national budget.
Preliminary effort estimations, based on UCPs method, give more than 13000 UCPs (Use Case Points), turning this project into one of the biggest development projects in the country with these characteristics.
Involved areas
The project has been structured in several areas: Functional Analysis, Architecture, Design & Development, Software Engineering, QA, Visual Design and Technical Support.
LIFIA is currently actively working in some of those areas while performing supervision and control activities over other suppliers.
Methodological support
The size of the system, the amount of involved personnel and the variety of required disciplines are factors that continously demand an effective organization. Within this context, the Software Engineering team has a critical role to play, choosing RUP as the basis for establishing guidelines and common practices.
Architecture
The architecture follows the SOA style, providing very low coupling between clients and server. The overall design heavily relies on the Object Oriented technology, since reuse and flexibility are required characteristics for the system under development.
Training
We are training the members of the Computer Science Unit very intensively to incorporate them in future stages of the development. This training includes more than 600hs of different courses and iterations.
Technology we are using
In tandem with the National Government policies, we are using Open Source Software where possible. It is important to note that currently almost every selected software belongs to that category:

• J2EE standard
• Oracle 10G database server
• Spring framework to handle middleware issues
• Hibernate O/R mapper
• Tapestry Web MVC framework
• Eclipse RCP platform
• JBoss application server
