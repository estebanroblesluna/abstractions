Corporate training
LIFIA exploits its professionals’ skills, who are generally professors who have been trained in Argentinean and foreign universities, in order to organize and give tailor-made courses and training workshops. Such courses are thought out in order to reach the recipient companies or enterprises’ expectations. The laboratory also offers programs with a curricula based on object orientation and data modeling which are, in turn, made of integrating courses that are incrementally given. These courses may be demanded individually or in accordance with the client’s needs.

Corporative training service
LIFIA’s training service offers three mechanisms oriented to different needs, these are expert courses, workshops and talks. Such courses generally last 16 hours and their main aim is to provide the trainees with theories, technologies and a great deal of examples of all kinds. Knowledge is dynamically transmitted by means of exercises to be solved and checked by means of group discussions. The workshops aim at generating concrete working experience. The instructor, in tandem with a group of collaborators according to the number of trainees, guides and assists the trainees. The workshops require availability of workspaces which allow the trainees to work individually or in pairs. The expert talks (lectures) aim at transmitting in a short period of time (generally a four-hour session) the key aspects of new technologies or successful cases in which they have been applied and provide valuable information for decision making; for example, for the management of technological change or the design of plans for Human Resources training.
