  Groupware systems  
Groupware applications allow a group of users to communicate with each other and work cooperatively. It means that the group can now coordinate their activities, solve problems, edit documents and diagrams, and so on. These capabilities are usually based on electronic services such as e-mails, video-conference, chat, etc.
As the popularity of the Internet continues to grow, millions of users are these days connected either to work, to learn, or just to communicate. Even more, several organizations are now integrating groupware technologies into their working environments. This situation requires new tools, environments and metholodogies to improve the way users collaborate to perform their tasks.
Collaborative environment offer different ways of collaboration, such as synchronic or asynchronic communication, face to face or remote work,etc.
