  Mobile & distributed systems  
The development of solutions for distributed systems requires a complete architectural vision in order to guarantee qualities such as high performance, robustness and flexibility, within a technological environment which continously evolves. The inclusion of mobile devices imposses new requirements to distributed systems developed in N-Tiers since the physical location of the user must be highly considered.
LIFIA's experience
Several succesfully executed projects (both national and international) give us a vast experience in this field, allowing us to tackle down complex projects related to the definition of distributed or mobile systems.
Services offered by LIFIA
Solutions development:
LIFIA carries out projects since their inception up to later implementation and deployment stages, applying the latest technologies and practices to ensure the quality of the results.
For your convenience, our teams can work "in house" as well as in our offices.

Training:
LIFIA´s highly trained professionals give corporate training courses related to distributed and/or mobile systems technologies and metodologies. The schedules and contents of these courses are usually agreed to better tailor them to the Client needs.
