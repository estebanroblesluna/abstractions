Consulting & mentoring
LIFIA has highly trained professionals, who apply the latest technologies and practices of the IT industry. They provide consulting and mentoring services to ensure an efective transfer of know-how and technology, adapting our processes and methodologies to fit our Customer's needs.
Solutions development

A significant number of research works and development projects have provided with a vast experience to define, develop and later support complex information systems. We staff our teams with experienced professionals to create high quality products. By means of rigourous software engineering methodologies we create strategic software, covering traditional areas as well as mobile, e-commerce and e-learning.
Corporate training

LIFIA uses all the experience of its professionals, which are generally also professors in national and international universities, to organize and to dictate courses and workshops specially tailored to the Customer´s needs.
We also have a curricula that covers the Object-Oriented Paradigm subject, which is made up of several short courses that are given incrementally. These courses can also be taken independently if they fit your needs.
