Impsat  
This company is one of the top telephony companies in Argentina and the region. It has been providing communication and data transfer services to other companies for more than ten years. In early 2002, we began providing consulting and developing services for several projects, which are described below:
Customers´ Website
You can find the Customers´ website here:
http://www.telefoniaimpsat.com
This site provides tools to Impsat´s Customers to manage their accounts (including calls details, invoicing and so on). Currently, only customers from Argentina and Peru are using this website (Brazilian customers will soon be included).
Centralized model
The objective of this project is to unify all the data sources defined by several distinct systems to later create a large datawarehouse.
Routing & Call costing administration
LIFIA developed several small applications to handle issues related to the interconnection with other telephony companies in order to select the best providers regarding the quality and cost of the services.
Revenue Assurance
The purpose of this project was to develop an application to calculate, as soon as possible, the revenue of every single product offered by the company.
Prepaid system
We developed this system to face Impsat´s need to offer prepaid modality to its Customers. By means of this application, we can control and administer almost every aspect of the communications, including real time costing.
Consulting and mentoring activities
We have provided consulting and mentoring services to support all our developed information systems (e.g. we have studied how to use and foster our Customer´s applications through the utilization of the Oracle Discoverer product).
Technologies we use
We have used several different technologies during the development stage, most of them J2EE applications with an Oracle backend. Different frameworks and products were also integrated (Struts, Hibernate, Apache Tomcat, CVS, Eclipse IDE and JfreeChart).
