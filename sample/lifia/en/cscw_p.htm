Recent Projects
CO-Protégé: A Groupware Tool for Supporting Collaborative Ontology Design with Divergence

Abstract:
Co-Protégé, is a platform to develop an ontology in a collaborative fashion, where people can carry out this collaborative activity by means of:
• using a private and a shared workspace
• supporting the discussion of alternative conceptualizations and
• being aware of the collaborative activity
