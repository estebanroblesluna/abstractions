Training course on OO and JEE for Movistar

  
Area
Corporate Training
Sub area / Vertical trade
Telecommunications
Period
November (2005) – August (2006)
Customer
Movistar
Project leader
Dr. Alejandro Fernández
Summary of the project
Since the beginning of 2005, several courses, workshops and lectures for members of Movistar’s staff of developers have been periodically given.
Objectives
Provide the members of Movistar’s development team with training on design-related issues, object-oriented development and JEE technology, particularly on web development.
Project´s scope

The number of trainees (already trained and to be trained): more than 40.
The courses, workshops and lectures which are periodically given and/or planned are:

• Introduction to objects
• Basic UML
• Object-oriented design
• Development of web applications with JSP, Servlets and Struts
• Workshop on Development of web applications with JSP, Servlets and Struts
• Lecture: Web Architectures
• Lecture: Persistence technologies in Web applications
• Lecture: Vertical Web Frameworks (Spring)
Technologies we used
JEE
Current status
The training course has recently finished with training on Web development.
