Founded in 1988 within the National University of La Plata, Argentina, LIFIA's capability for conceiving, designing, developing and implementing systems in a variety of national and international institutions and companies, including finance, communications, government, health, education and the software industry has been widely acknowledged.
We combine our background and experience in Software Engineering, Formal Methods and other software disciplines to create solutions for our clients in "Internet time". We have implemented modern Web software, GIS applications, embedded gaming systems, software for health organizations and other kind of advanced systems.
