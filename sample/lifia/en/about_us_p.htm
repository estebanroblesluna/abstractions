Philosophy

LIFIA is a research laboratory that offers the national and international academic and business communities its IT knowledge and services.
Founded in 1988 within the National University of La Plata, Argentina, LIFIA's capability for conceiving, designing and implementing informations systems has been widely acknowledged.


Objectives

The development of high quality, basic and applied research in different scientific areas and it’s.

LIFIA mainly promotes the development of research projects which, in turn, effectively contribute to the development of the development of methods, techniques and products in the area of it’s and which can be transferred both to the domestic and international industry.

The promotion of highly trained human resources in he areas of research and it development.

LIFIA’s members are professors, researchers, professionals and university students with vast experience in research, development, implementation and training in it.

The transference of technology and training in it to the industry and to the society in general terms.

• LIFIA will promote the application of the result of its research works as well as the participation of its members in the solution of actual technological problems both in the it industry and the domestic as well as the international environment..
• LIFIA will promote the productive innovation through the application of methods, techniques and tools with the highes level to the solution of actual industrial problesms both in argentina and abroad..
