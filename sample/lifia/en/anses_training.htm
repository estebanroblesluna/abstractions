OO training course for Anses  
 
Area
Corporate Training
Sub area/ vertical trade
Public Administration
Period
June 2006- July (2007) ongoing in 2007
Customer
Anses - http://www.anses.gov.ar/
Project leader
Dr. Alejandro Fernández
Summary of the project
Object-Oriented technological training for the Anses’s developing team. Planned with several iterations.
Objectives
Providing assistants with the essential concepts related to design based on the Object-Oriented paradigm, modelling languages such as UML with some notions on process such as RUP and introduction to Web services and programming under the .NET platform.
Project´s scope
The total number of trainees reaches 100 people (this figure covers those who have already received the training course and those who are to receive it in the near future).
Topics and Technologies:
• Introduction to Objects
• Design with Objects
• Basic UML
• Object-Oriented design with patterns
• Object-Oriented Design applying UML
• Introduction to Web Servicies
• Basic .NET
Current status
About to finish the first course iteration. The future ones have already been planned: 8 more iterations to train a total of 100 Anses’s developers.
