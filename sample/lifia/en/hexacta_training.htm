O.O. and J2EE Corporate training at Hexacta  
Area
Corporate training
Vertical Markets
IT
Execution time frame
May - July (2005)
Customer
Hexacta S.A.
Project leader
Dr. Alejandro Fernández
Project´s brief
Corporate training covering O.O. fundamentals. The audience was primarily composed by junior developers of Hexacta S.A.
Objectives
Instructing assistants with the mininal and essentials concepts of the Object Oriented Design practices and techniques.
Project´s scope
Several iterations have been given, each one consisting of the following courses:
• Introduction to OOD
• OOD workshops
• Introduction to Design Patterns
• Design patterns workshops
Technologies we used
Smalltalk
Results / Perspectives
Results were highly satisfactory.
Current status
We have recently finished the last planned iteration.
