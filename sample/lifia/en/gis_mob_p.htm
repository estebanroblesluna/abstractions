GIS and mobile computing

In this project we do research to find answers to the problems found when building GIS and mobile applications. Our interest is primarily in the software engineering aspects, focusing in architectural and modelling methods issues.

Research results transfer
Our objective in this area is to apply software engineering practices and methodologies in the construction of GIS software. We are now working in cooperation with an international Customer to adjust GPS positions to well known vehicle paths for their tracking and monitoring.
